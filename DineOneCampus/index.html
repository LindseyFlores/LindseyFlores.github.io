Dine Near Campus

Sometimes you aren’t in the mood for the UNCG caf’s food, but when it comes to dining near campus, finding the right place to spend your money can be hard decision. The last thing anyone likes is knowing you just spent $20 on a plate of disappointing food.

So welcome to Dine Near Campus!

Here you will be presented with some of my favorite spots to eat at near UNCG, all of which are affordable and consist of great food. Ive curated a meal plan that allows you too see some of the top dishes at each establishment and give you an estimate cost of what you will be spending by eating there.
